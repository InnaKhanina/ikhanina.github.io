What It Is About

Memories are not eternal, things get forgotten, diaries got lost and there are fewer and fewer things you can recap in your 80s. Fortunately,
nowadays you can keep your memories in a digital format so you do not tell everyone the same story of how did you apply to the university,
send pictures to all your family members out of all the places you have been to. You can create one place where you would share all stories,
pictures, videos about the events happening in your life so everyone who wants could see that. This website is a personal space of mine
where I, Inna Khanina, an ambitious girl in her early 20s studying abroad, traveling and exploring
the world from different perspectives will be sharing my life journey.
